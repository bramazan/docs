## Platform Capabilities

### Payment Processing
- **Non-3D & 3D Secure**: Full support for secure card payments
- **Pre-Authorization**: Hold funds before capture
- **Installments**: Flexible installment options
- **Refunds & Voids**: Complete transaction lifecycle management

### Multi-Currency & Localization
- **Currency Support**: TRY, USD, EUR
- **Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC)**: Let cardholders pay in their card currency
- **Exchange Rate Management**: Real-time forex rates

### Marketplace Features
- **Recursive Sub-merchants**: Unlimited hierarchy depth
- **Split Payments**: Automated fund distribution
- **Commission Management**: Flexible fee structures
- **Individual Settlements**: Per-merchant payouts

### Security & Compliance
- **PCI DSS Level 1**: Fully compliant infrastructure
- **GDPR & KVKK**: Data privacy compliance
- **Encryption**: At rest and in transit
- **Audit Logs**: Complete activity tracking

### Developer Experience
- **RESTful API**: Clean, intuitive endpoints
- **Webhooks**: Real-time event notifications
- **SDKs**: Coming soon (JavaScript, PHP, Python, C#)
- **Comprehensive Documentation**: What you're reading now!

## Architecture

Our platform is built on modern microservices architecture:

- **API Gateway**: Rate limiting, authentication, routing
- **Payment Engine**: Core payment processing
- **Bank Connectors**: 7 Tier-1 Turkish banks integrated
- **Smart Routing**: Intelligent connector selection
- **Ledger Integration**: Formance-based accounting
- **Webhook System**: Svix-powered notifications
